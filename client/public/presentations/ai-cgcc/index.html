Story 1 — Operations

A trucking company with 10 trucks.

When I started working with this company, I identified many problems. One of the biggest was in the operations department.

This is a weight ticket.

Every single load requires one. Handwritten. Every time.

Now multiply that by thousands.

10 trucks. Multiple loads per day. Over a month, hundreds, sometimes thousands of weight tickets.

Every one has to be logged. Net weight calculated. Then sent out as an invoice to the client.

The invoicing for a single month could take three months to complete. Weight Tickets miscalculated. Sent back. Redone.

Because of this bottleneck,
the owner didn't want more trucks.

Not because of demand. Not because of drivers.
Because the paperwork was killing the business.

The Solution

One photo. 98% accuracy. Instant.

Instead of carrying paper to the office, the driver takes a photo. Stamped with date and time automatically.

AI vision analyzes the ticket instantly, 98% accuracy. The driver reviews the extracted data, confirms with one tap, and submits to the database.

From photo to invoice. Automated.

Photo → AI extraction → Database → QuickBooks → Invoice to client.

The result

Hundreds of hours of admin work per year reduced to minutes per week. After a quick verification from the office, the automated invoice goes out.

Everything in one place.

Every ticket, every truck, every weight. Searchable instantly. Real-time visibility.
